REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL — STUCCO & EXTERIOR FINISH SYSTEMS
Project: Kahala Park Townhomes (Building C)
Owner: Kahala Park Development LLC
GC: Pacific Constructors Hawaii
RFP #: PCH-2026-0411
Issue date: 2026-05-04
Bid deadline: 2026-05-22 5:00 PM HST

1.0 PROJECT DESCRIPTION

Kahala Park Townhomes Building C is a three-story, 12-unit residential
building located at 4567 Kahala Avenue, Honolulu, HI 96816. Building C
follows the same architectural language as Buildings A and B (completed
2024-2025).

Subcontractors are invited to provide a lump-sum bid for the exterior
stucco and finish system as described herein.

2.0 SCOPE

2.1 Conventional three-coat cement plaster system to all exterior walls
    not specifically called out for other finish (approximately 4,800
    sqft per drawings A-201 through A-206).

2.2 Acrylic textured finish coat, manufacturer-standard color per owner
    selection (provided at construction start).

2.3 All required metal lath, weather-resistive barrier coordination,
    accessories per system manufacturer's published details.

2.4 Bidder shall include:
    - Mobilization and setup
    - All scaffolding required for the work
    - Daily cleanup of stucco work areas
    - One mockup panel (4'x8') for owner approval prior to full
      production

3.0 SUBMITTAL REQUIREMENTS

Bidder shall submit:
- Lump-sum price for the work as described
- Schedule of values showing labor, material, equipment, mobilization
- Proposed schedule (start date, duration in calendar days)
- Manufacturer-approved system specification
- Three references from comparable Hawaii projects completed within
  the last 24 months
- Certificates of insurance (general liability $2M/$4M, workers comp
  Hawaii-statutory, automobile $1M)

4.0 SCHEDULE

Notice to Proceed: 2026-06-15 (target)
Substantial completion: 2026-09-30
Liquidated damages: $500/day per the prime contract

5.0 SITE CONDITIONS

5.1 Site access via Kahala Avenue only; staging area shown on plan G-001
5.2 No on-site material storage beyond two weeks of production
5.3 Working hours 7:00 AM - 5:00 PM Monday through Saturday
5.4 Power and water provided by GC at no cost to subcontractor

6.0 EXCLUSIONS BY GC (subcontractor not responsible for):

- Structural framing, sheathing, weather-resistive barrier installation
- Sheet metal flashings (by separate sub)
- Painting beyond integral finish color
- Final cleanup of glass and adjacent finishes
- Permits and fees (held by GC)
